Our Services

The Business is working in importation, supply of drugs and medical consumable to Government, Private hospitals and Non Governmental Organization, also do retails. It also focuses on doing other enterprise like dealer Cosmetics, Laboratory services and equiptment, Hospital services and Car dealing.

Home Medical Equiptment

Kpatama Pharmaceutical company is a multi-oriented Company when it comes to the matter of medical equiptment, we do import and do whole sale in all kind of medical equiptment ranging from Gynecological, Cesarean kit, Minor and General surgery kit of all types of surgeries. Orthopedic surgical kit.

Respiratiory Services

With the Doctors, CHOs, Nurses and Pharmacist we have, we don't only sell drugs to patient or client but we give professional advise thereby advising patients to with respiratory condition to carryout all necessary chest and lungs examination. We send patients to our associated Hospital where they will be check for any kind of respiratory condition.

Cosmetic Goods

We don't only cater for healthy conditon, we do also cater for the physical make-up of mankind. The beauty and handsomeness of human living in society is peramount to us. With these, we sell all kind of natural and artifical products from Natures that will help fight aging process of human and at the same time, help the skin stay healthy and young.

Car Dealer

The company is a multi task business oriented. We care for the life of everyone by making sure that basic amenities are met and people are able to work between healthy status and societal involvment by making sure they are able to reach their destination as quick as possible. We sell Toyota, Benz, Rav 4, Landcruser, Prado and many more.
